AniPong Privacy Policy

The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policy about handling data. Below are the links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Storage (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Replicate AI Services

Image Processing

When you use our AI photo conversion feature, your uploaded images are:

  1. Temporarily stored on Firebase Storage (Google Cloud Platform)
  2. Processed by Replicate AI services for style conversion
  3. Automatically deleted from our systems after processing

These third-party services process your images solely for the purpose of providing the AI conversion functionality and do not retain or use your images for any other purpose.

Data Retention

  • Uploaded images are temporarily stored on Firebase Storage during processing
  • Images are automatically deleted from our systems within 24 hours of processing completion
  • Replicate AI processes images for conversion purposes only and does not retain images for training or other purposes (per their privacy policy)
  • In case of technical issues, images are automatically purged from our storage within 7 days maximum
